The old man sat on the park bench, watching the children play. They were laughing and shouting as they ran around, carefree and happy. He smiled, remembering his own childhood, when things had been much simpler. He had seen so much change over the years, so much progress and growth. But sometimes, he longed for the past, for the days when life was slower and quieter. He closed his eyes and breathed in the fresh air, feeling the warmth of the sun on his skin.
